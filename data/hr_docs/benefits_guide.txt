BENEFITS GUIDE 2024

HEALTH AND WELLNESS BENEFITS

Medical Insurance Plans
TechCorp offers three medical insurance options through BlueCross BlueShield:

Standard Plan (PPO)
- Monthly premium: $150 employee, $450 family
- Deductible: $1,500 individual, $3,000 family
- Out-of-pocket maximum: $6,000 individual, $12,000 family
- Office visits: $25 copay
- Urgent care: $50 copay
- Emergency room: $250 copay

Premium Plan (PPO)
- Monthly premium: $225 employee, $675 family
- Deductible: $500 individual, $1,000 family
- Out-of-pocket maximum: $3,500 individual, $7,000 family
- Office visits: $15 copay
- Urgent care: $30 copay
- Emergency room: $150 copay

High Deductible Plan (HDHP)
- Monthly premium: $75 employee, $225 family
- Deductible: $3,000 individual, $6,000 family
- Out-of-pocket maximum: $6,900 individual, $13,800 family
- HSA eligible with company contribution
- Preventive care covered 100%

Dental Insurance
Delta Dental PPO coverage included:
- Preventive care: 100% covered
- Basic procedures: 80% covered after $50 deductible
- Major procedures: 50% covered after $50 deductible
- Annual maximum benefit: $2,000
- Orthodontic coverage: 50% up to $2,000 lifetime maximum

Vision Insurance
VSP vision plan includes:
- Annual eye exam: $15 copay
- Frames: $150 allowance every 2 years
- Lenses: Covered in full with copays
- Contact lenses: $150 allowance annually
- Laser surgery discounts available

FINANCIAL BENEFITS

401(k) Retirement Plan
Fidelity manages our 401(k) plan with these features:
- Immediate enrollment eligibility
- Company match: 50% of contributions up to 6% of salary
- Vesting: Immediate for employee contributions, 3-year cliff for company match
- Investment options: 15 mutual funds plus target-date funds
- Loans available up to 50% of vested balance

Employee Stock Purchase Plan
Purchase company stock at 15% discount:
- Quarterly purchase periods
- Payroll deduction from 1% to 10% of salary
- 2-year holding period required
- No fees for purchases

Life and Disability Insurance
Basic life insurance: 2x annual salary provided at no cost
Supplemental life insurance: Up to 5x salary available for purchase
Accidental death coverage: Equal to life insurance amount

Short-term disability: 60% of salary for up to 6 months
Long-term disability: 60% of salary after 6 months
Coverage begins on first day of employment

ENROLLMENT AND CHANGES

Open Enrollment
Annual period: November 1-30
Coverage begins: January 1
Required for all benefit changes except qualifying events

Qualifying Life Events
Marriage or divorce within 30 days
Birth or adoption within 60 days
Loss of other coverage within 60 days
Change in employment status
Spouse employment changes

New Employee Enrollment
Benefits effective: First day of month following 30 days of employment
Enrollment deadline: 30 days from hire date
Default elections: Basic life and disability insurance only
HR support: Personal benefits counseling available
