EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK

Welcome to TechCorp! This handbook contains important information about company policies and procedures.

EMPLOYMENT POLICIES

Work Schedule
Our standard work week is Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Flexible scheduling is available with manager approval. Remote work is permitted up to 3 days per week for eligible employees.

Time Off Policy
All full-time employees receive:
- 15 days paid vacation in year one
- 20 days paid vacation after 3 years
- 25 days paid vacation after 5 years
- 10 sick days annually
- 12 company holidays
- Personal days: 3 per year

Vacation requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal. Unused vacation days up to 5 can be carried over to the next year.

Benefits Package
Health Insurance: Company pays 80% of employee premiums, 60% for family coverage
Dental and Vision: 100% employee, 75% family coverage
401(k): Company matches 50% of contributions up to 6% of salary
Life Insurance: 2x annual salary provided at no cost
Disability Insurance: Short-term and long-term coverage included
Professional Development: $2,000 annual allowance for training and conferences

PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT

Performance Reviews
Annual performance reviews are conducted each December. Mid-year check-ins occur in June. Performance is evaluated on:
- Goal achievement
- Technical competency
- Collaboration and teamwork
- Leadership potential
- Company value alignment

Promotion Process
Promotions are based on performance, potential, and business needs. Employees may request promotion consideration during their annual review. Requirements include:
- Consistent meets or exceeds expectations ratings
- Demonstrated leadership or technical growth
- Positive peer feedback
- Manager recommendation

WORKPLACE CONDUCT

Dress Code
Business casual attire is standard. On Fridays, casual dress is permitted. Client-facing roles may require business professional attire. Remote workers should maintain professional appearance for video calls.

Anti-Harassment Policy
TechCorp maintains a zero-tolerance policy for harassment, discrimination, or retaliation. All employees have the right to a respectful workplace. Report concerns to HR immediately.

Communication Guidelines
Professional communication is expected in all interactions. Use company email and chat platforms appropriately. Social media posts should not reference company confidential information.

TECHNOLOGY POLICIES

Equipment
Company laptops are provided to all employees. Personal use is permitted within reasonable limits. Software installation requires IT approval. Equipment must be returned upon termination.

Data Security
All employees must complete annual security training. Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication. Never share login credentials. Report security incidents immediately.

Remote Work Technology
VPN access is mandatory for remote work. Use company-approved cloud storage only. Ensure home Wi-Fi networks are secure. Video calls require professional backgrounds.

LEAVE POLICIES

Family Leave
Eligible employees may take up to 12 weeks unpaid leave under FMLA. New parents receive 6 weeks paid leave. Adoption leave follows the same policy as parental leave.

Medical Leave
Extended medical leave may be granted with proper documentation. Return-to-work clearance required from healthcare provider. Reasonable accommodations provided as needed.

Bereavement Leave
5 paid days for immediate family members, 3 days for extended family. Additional unpaid time may be approved on case-by-case basis.

COMPENSATION

Salary Structure
Salaries are reviewed annually and adjusted based on performance, market rates, and budget. Pay grades ensure internal equity. Overtime is available for non-exempt employees.

Bonus Programs
Annual bonuses based on company and individual performance. Target bonus percentages vary by role level. Spot bonuses available for exceptional contributions.

Expense Reimbursement
Business expenses require pre-approval and receipts. Submit through expense portal within 30 days. Common reimbursable expenses include travel, meals, and professional memberships.

PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT

Training Opportunities
Internal training programs cover technical skills, leadership, and personal development. External conference attendance encouraged. Lunch-and-learn sessions held monthly.

Tuition Reimbursement
Company reimburses 75% of approved coursework costs up to $5,000 annually. Must maintain grade B or higher. Courses must be job-related or support career advancement.

Mentorship Program
Formal mentoring program pairs employees with senior colleagues. Program includes structured goals and regular check-ins. Participation is voluntary and career-enhancing.

TERMINATION PROCEDURES

Resignation
Two weeks notice required for professional roles. One week notice for hourly positions. Exit interviews conducted to gather feedback. Final paycheck includes accrued vacation.

Termination for Cause
Immediate termination may result from policy violations, misconduct, or performance issues after progressive discipline. Final paycheck excludes vacation payout in some cases.

Company Property
All company equipment, keys, and materials must be returned. Access to company systems disabled on last day. Return shipping provided for remote employees.
